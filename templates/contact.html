About This Website

This website provides real-time weather and air quality information. The data is collected from the following trusted sources:

  • OpenWeather API: Provides accurate and up-to-date weather forecasts and air quality data.
  • Weather API: Offers detailed historical weather data for various locations worldwide.

The aim of this website is to deliver reliable information to help you make informed decisions based on current weather conditions and air quality levels in your area.
